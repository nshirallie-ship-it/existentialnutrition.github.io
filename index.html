Nutrition got you feeling existential?

Well, welome to health! Because without health...there's nothing! After staring into the void (aka the mirror) a little too long, I decided to join the real world in search for answers. There I found the power of choice, and the foundation of nutrition. Our bodies are incredible, resilient, but there's sooo much that can go wrong (unfortunately). I find that when I feel healthy, I feel better prepared to take on whatever life has to offer. And while I'm here...might as well make the most of it! Existential Nutrition is perfect for you if you:

  • need help regulating blood sugar
  • need help meal planning
  • need help managing chronic illness through diet
  • or just need/want support for health and nutrition woes!

What can you expect at Existential Nutrition?

Besides some existential dread, you can expect a lighthearted, casual tone! I believe that attitude can chage a lot, so I emphasize humor when I can. Existential Nutrition isn't soley about nutrition, although it is my focus. I want to enourage everyone to do the best they can with where they are now. My focus is on creating healthy, sustainable habits. There's no such thing as perfection, and isn't that boring anyway? But I believe that your life, begins with your health, and feeling as healthy, confidant, and amazing as you can! This is where I can help:

  1. if you've been struggling with putting together meals that fit your nutrition requirements as well as your time constraints
  2. feeling healthy in your body with chronic disease
  3. struggling to accept your body
  4. or just need some accountability and support
...I'm here for your nutritional inqueries and support!

My Diet Philosophy

Well, besides feeling existential about it that is....
I believe in an individualistic approach to nutrition, so what works for me, may not be the best fit for you! And that's ok. Every body is different, and it's embracing these differences that can help clarify what could be the missing piece in your personal nutrition puzzle. I believe on focusing on unprocessed foods as much as possible, while still enjoying convenience when desired. One thing I enjoy about existentialism (and why it's so relevant to my personal philosophy) is that it conveys the importance of personal choice and responsilbilty. Ultimately, we choose how we live our lives, including our diets! But sometimes we need a little help...(right?)
With that, I'm happy to be a part of your journey in figuring out how best to achieve your health goals...for the health of it!

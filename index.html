What can you expect at Existential Nutrition?

Besides some existential dread, you can expect a lighthearted, casual tone! I believe that attitude can chage a lot, so I emphasize humor when I can. This website isn't soley about nutrition, although it is my focus. I want to enourage everyone to do the best they can with where they are now. There's no such thing as perfection, and isn't that boring anyway? But I believe that your life, begins with your health, and feeling as healthy, confidant, and amazing as you can! This is where I ome in, if you've been struggling with putting together meals that fit your nutrition requirements as well as your time constraints, feeling good in your body with chronic disease, struggling to accept your body, or just need some accountability and support, I'm your girl!

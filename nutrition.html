Sugar-free chocolate chip cookie

Sometimes you just want a sweet treat, but don't want all the sugar...this is my fav cookie recipe: BIG single serving, crispy edges, chewy, delicious!

  • 1 Tablespoon butter (can sub vegan butter)
  • 2 Tablespoon sweetner (I like allulose)
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1 Tablespoon tapioca flour/starch
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 3-4 Tablespoon sugar free chocolate chips (Lily's brand is a good choice)

Combine almond flour, tapioca starch, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl. In a separate mixing bowl, soften butter in microwave for 10 seconds. Add sweetner and cream together until combined and fluffy. Mix in vanilla. Stir in the combined flours/salt mixture. It will take a minute until it is combined and holds together like a cookie dough. Add in chocolate chips. Form into 1 large or 2 smaller cookie scoops (I used my hands at this point). Do not flatten and place on parchment paper. Bake @350 degree F for 15-20 min or until edges are brown and center is solid. Take out of oven/toaster oven and let cool on parchment for 10-15 minutes. Enjoy!
